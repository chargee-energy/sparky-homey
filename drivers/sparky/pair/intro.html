Requires the latest firmware version

Use these steps to confirm you're on the latest firmware version.

Follow these steps to check your Sparky firmware version:

  1. Connect your Sparky device to a power source.
  2. Open the Sparky app on your smartphone.
  3. Navigate to Profile > Your address > Sparky .
  4. Ensure your Sparky is running on firmware version 52 or higher before you continue

Note: If your Sparky device is not running on the latest firmware, please update it before proceeding. This happens within 24 hours of installation automatically. If you need help, please contact our support team.
